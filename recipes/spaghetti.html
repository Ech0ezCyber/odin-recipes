Classic Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
  4. Taste and adjust the seasoning as needed.
  5. Serve the cooked spaghetti topped with the tomato sauce. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Enjoy your delicious homemade spaghetti!
