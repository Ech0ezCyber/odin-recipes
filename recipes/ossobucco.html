Osso Buco Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of veal shank
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken or beef broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions:

  1. Season the veal shanks with salt and pepper, then dredge them in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Brown the veal shanks on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Saut√© until the vegetables are softened and golden.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  5. Deglaze the skillet with the white wine, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom.
  6. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken or beef broth, bay leaf, and dried thyme. Stir to combine.
  7. Return the browned veal shanks to the skillet, submerging them in the liquid.
  8. Cover the skillet and reduce the heat to low. Simmer for about 2 to 2.5 hours, or until the meat is tender and falls off the bone.
  9. Remove the bay leaf before serving.

Serve the Osso Buco with gremolata and your favorite side dish.
